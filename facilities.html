Facilities

Resort Facilities

Beyond rooms, dining, and spa, Château de Remmy is a self-contained resort. Every facility below is included in your stay and accessible to all guests from check-in to check-out.

Infinity Pool

A heated infinity pool overlooking the Mediterranean, open year-round with a poolside bar.

Fitness Center

A 24-hour gym with strength equipment, free weights, and daily guided classes.

Private Beach

Direct access to a secluded stretch of coastline, with loungers and full beach service.

Kids Club

Supervised activities for ages 4–12, open daily from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Pet-Friendly Stays

Well-behaved pets are welcome in select rooms, with bedding and bowls provided.

Valet Parking

Complimentary valet and secure underground parking for all overnight guests.

24-Hour Concierge

From restaurant bookings to private excursions, our concierge is on call around the clock.

Laundry & Pressing

Same-day laundry, dry cleaning, and pressing services available on request.

Private Yacht Charter

Half-day and full-day charters along the Riviera coastline, arranged through the concierge.
