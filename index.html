A Legacy of Elegance on the Riviera

Nestled along the sun-drenched coastline, Château de Remmy has welcomed travelers since 1898 with a quiet, uncompromising sense of grace.

Every detail, from hand-laid stone corridors to the scent of lavender drifting through open courtyards, has been preserved and renewed across four generations of family ownership. Here, hospitality is not a service. It is a tradition.
