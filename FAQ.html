How Can We Help?

You can book directly through our Reservations page, selecting your dates, room type, and guest details. Our team will confirm availability and send a confirmation email within 24 hours. You can also call our reservations line directly for assistance.

Most bookings require a one-night deposit at the time of confirmation, with the balance due upon arrival. Suites and villas booked during peak season may require a 50% deposit. Full details are provided at checkout.

Yes. Reservations can be modified or cancelled free of charge up to 7 days before arrival. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a one-night charge. Please contact our reservations team directly for any changes.

Check-in begins at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can often be arranged on request, subject to availability, at no extra cost for suite and villa guests.

Well-behaved pets are welcome in select Deluxe Rooms and the Private Villa. Please notify us at the time of booking so we can prepare bedding and bowls. A small additional cleaning fee applies.

Yes. We offer step-free access to the main building, pool deck, and Le Jardin restaurant, along with accessible rooms fitted with roll-in showers. Please let us know your requirements when booking.

Yes, reservations at L'Azure are strongly recommended, particularly during high season when tables are limited to 40 covers per night. Hotel guests can reserve directly through the concierge.

Absolutely. Both restaurants accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. We recommend noting any restrictions when booking your table or your room.

Guests must be 18 years or older to book and check in without a parent or guardian present. Families with children of any age are welcome when accompanied by an adult.

Château de Remmy is a smoke-free property. Designated outdoor smoking areas are available in the gardens and on select private terraces. A cleaning fee applies for smoking inside rooms.

Yes, complimentary valet parking and secure underground parking are included for all overnight guests, with no additional charge for the duration of your stay.
