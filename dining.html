Culinary Experiences

Dining at the Château

A Table With a View

Two Restaurants, One Philosophy

Every dish served at Château de Remmy begins with what the Riviera offers that morning — the catch from Nice's harbor, herbs from our own garden, and produce from growers we have worked with for decades. Two distinct restaurants share this same conviction, each with its own rhythm and setting.

Signature Restaurant

L'Azure

Modern French cuisine with Mediterranean influence, served on a terrace suspended above the sea.

Open for dinner only, L'Azure seats just 40 guests a night across an intimate dining room and an open-air terrace. Tasting menus change weekly depending on what the markets and our own gardens provide, with a wine list curated from small Provençal and Burgundian producers.

  • Dinner, 7:00 PM – 11:00 PM
  • Smart elegant dress code
  • Tasting menu & à la carte
All-Day Dining

Le Jardin

A relaxed courtyard restaurant for breakfast, lunch, and casual evenings under the olive trees.

Le Jardin is where the Château gathers throughout the day — fresh pastries and Riviera breakfasts each morning, light Mediterranean plates at lunch, and shared boards for evenings spent lingering at the table. Children and families are always welcome here.

  • 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM, daily
  • Casual, resort attire
  • Breakfast, lunch & light dinner

Executive Chef

Antoine Lefèvre

"Cooking on the Riviera means listening to the season before deciding on the menu."

Chef Antoine trained under two Michelin-starred kitchens in Lyon and Marseille before joining Château de Remmy in 2015. His approach favors restraint over spectacle, letting the quality of local ingredients lead every plate that leaves the kitchen. Under his direction, L'Azure has been recognized in regional culinary guides for four consecutive years.
