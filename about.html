Our Story

About Château de Remmy

Since 1898

A Family Legacy on the Riviera

Château de Remmy was built as a private residence in 1898 and opened its doors to guests for the first time in 1924.

What began as a single villa overlooking the Baie des Anges has grown, across four generations of family ownership, into one of the Riviera's most quietly celebrated hotels. We have resisted the temptation to grow too large or too loud — every renovation has been undertaken to preserve the character of the original building, not replace it. Today, the same family that welcomed its first guests a century ago still oversees every detail of the experience.

Our Mission

To offer a standard of hospitality that feels personal, never performed — where every guest is known, not just checked in.

Our Vision

To remain, for the next hundred years, a place where the Riviera's quiet elegance is preserved rather than reinvented.

Our History

A Century of Milestones

1898

The Villa is Built

Constructed as a private residence for the Remmy family overlooking the Baie des Anges.

1924

First Guests Welcomed

The villa opens its doors to outside guests for the first time, with just twelve rooms.

1961

The Ballroom Wing

The Grand Ballroom is added, quickly becoming a fixture of Riviera social life.

1998

Centennial Restoration

A full restoration honors the original architecture while modernizing every guest room.

2015

Spa de Remmy Opens

The château's wine cellar is transformed into a wellness sanctuary beneath the stone vaults.

2024

A Century of Hospitality

Château de Remmy celebrates 100 years of welcoming guests, still under family ownership.
