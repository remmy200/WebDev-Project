About Château de Remmy

Since 1898

A Family Legacy on the Riviera

Château de Remmy was built as a private residence in 1898 and opened its doors to guests for the first time in 1924.

What began as a single villa overlooking the Baie des Anges has grown, across four generations of family ownership, into one of the Riviera's most quietly celebrated hotels. We have resisted the temptation to grow too large or too loud — every renovation has been undertaken to preserve the character of the original building, not replace it. Today, the same family that welcomed its first guests a century ago still oversees every detail of the experience.
